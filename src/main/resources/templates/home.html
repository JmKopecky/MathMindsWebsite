Our Focus Is STEM

Math and problem-solving skills are vital in today's world. The earlier that these abilities can be developed, the stronger they can grow to be. That's where we come in.

Sure, But Who Are We?

MathMinds is a student-led non-profit organization. We started as a club at Goose Creek Memorial high school. But we aren't content with just being a club.

Call Us Ambitious

To achieve our goal, we seek to collaborate with educators and schools. In or outside our district; expanding offers ever-increasing opportunities to help students.

We're Not Just Talk

Competition breeds not only innovation, but motivation. And when your market consists of young students, motivation is vital to fueling the spark of intelligence.
